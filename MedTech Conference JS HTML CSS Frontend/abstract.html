Call for Abstracts - Health CodeX Workshop 2024

We invite professionals, researchers, and students to submit their abstracts for the Health CodeX Workshop 2024. Showcase your work and contribute to advancements in medical data standards and healthcare technologies.

Submission Guidelines:

  • Abstracts should not exceed 300 words.
  • Must include title, authors, and affiliations.
  • Submissions should be in PDF format.

Important Deadlines:

  • Submission Deadline: October 1, 2024
